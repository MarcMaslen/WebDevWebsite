About Us

My name is Marc, I am a University graduate with a Computer Science degree. I started this business because one of my favorite hobbies is to create, design and generally mess around making websites. Most of my spare time has been spent working towards bettering myself as a developer, teaching myself the best practices to build websites and write code myself. I am the sole designer, developer and am the owner here at Blue Zero Web Designs


When I was looking around at some websites for local businesses, I could see a lot of issues with them visually and with their functionality. After suggesting some fixes to these businesses for their websites. I found that most, if not all of them didn't have the funds to have a web developer working on their site. This showed me that there was a need for small businesses to have a web developer in order to help keep there website visually looking great and functioning correctly for a reasonable price.
